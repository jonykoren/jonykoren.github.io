About

From the first moment that I have learnt the Machine Learning fundamentals, I realized that this is the field that attracts me the most: a combination of statistics, math and programming, along with in-depth and constant learning of state-of-the-art architectures and models in order to lead the technology of tomorrow.

In my master’s degree, i was exposed to many fields such as autonomous systems, web and data mining, machine and deep learning, social network analysis, computer vision and natural language processing.

My master’s thesis dealt with improving malignancy prediction in medical CT scans using deep convolutional networks.

I work as a freelancer for one year and providing Data Science services to clients from all over the world.
These days I am starting a new job as a Machine Learning Engineer at a startup company.

Areas of Interest:

  • Machine Learning
  • Deep Learning and especially Computer Vision
  • Data Mining
  • Data-Driven Analytics
  • Data Visualization
